CardVerify respects your privacy. We process your gift card number and PIN solely to retrieve balance information from the respective brand and do not store sensitive card data.

- We do not sell or share your personal information.
- We do not persist your card number or PIN after verification.
- Optional local history is stored only in your browser and can be cleared at any time.

If you have questions, please contact us at support@cardverify.org.
